# Interview Prep: Cav – Junior Fullstack + AI Developer

## Job Overview
Cav is hiring a Junior Fullstack + AI Developer to build and maintain features on their Compliance OS™ platform—an agentic AI system that automates, validates, and monitors cyber-compliance controls. - Frontend work in React (or Vue/Angular)  
- Backend services in Python, Node.js, or Java  
- AI integrations (LLM agents, vector DBs, prompt orchestration)  
- Database design (PostgreSQL & MongoDB)  
- DevOps tasks (Docker, CI/CD, AWS)

- **Domain Interest**: Although new to formal compliance, your hunger for structured, mission-critical systems aligns with Cav’s continuous assurance mission.
